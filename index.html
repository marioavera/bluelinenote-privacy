Privacy Policy

BlueLineNote does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

All information entered into the app remains on the user’s device and is not transmitted to any external server. The developer does not have access to notes, reports, scans, or any user-generated content.

BlueLineNote does not provide cloud storage, remote databases, or long-term external data retention. Users may export or delete their data at any time.

Users are responsible for securing their device and for how they store, manage, and use any information entered into the app.

The app uses Apple services such as in-app purchases and speech recognition, which are handled by Apple under their own privacy policies.
